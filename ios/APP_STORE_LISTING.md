## Description

STONE is a free 2-player strategy board game inspired by ancient temple games. Roll the dice, move your stones, capture opponents, and race to bear off all 13 pieces first!

ROLL THE JESTER DICE
Each die has a Jester face replacing the 6. Roll a Jester with any number and you get quadruple moves! Roll double Jesters for ultimate control — choose any value for your doubles.

PLAY YOUR WAY
- Online Multiplayer: Challenge friends or strangers with invite links
- VS Computer: 4 AI difficulty levels from Easy to Expert
- Pass & Play: 2-player local games on one device

COMPETE FOR GLORY
- Player of the Month competition with exclusive champion rewards
- Leaderboards tracking wins, streaks, and coins
- Wager coins on games for higher stakes

CUSTOMIZE YOUR GAME
- 40+ unique stone colors including NFL team colors
- 8 board themes from Marble Palace to Lava Rock
- Custom profile pictures and avatars

STRATEGIC DEPTH
- 13 stones per player on a 20-space diamond board
- Capture lone opponents and send them to the Stoned Dungeon
- Crown your pieces in the home stretch for the final push
- Manage your dice wisely — doubles give 4 moves!

Free to play. No ads. No pay-to-win.
